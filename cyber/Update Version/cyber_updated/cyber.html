How to play the game

Controls:
  • Mouse Rotation: Use the mouse to navigate and rotate within the scene.
  • Object Interaction: Point the cursor and left-click to grab an object.
  • Throw Objects: Press the SPACE BAR to throw objects like paper planes and other items.
  • Placing Clues: To place a clue in the correct spot, point towards the flashing objects. Scroll up to bring them closer or scroll down to move them further away. The object will automatically snap into place when it is close enough to the holder.
  • Dialogue Progression: Press ENTER to continue through the dialogues.
